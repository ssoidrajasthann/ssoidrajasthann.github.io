What is SSO Rajasthan?

The SSO Rajasthan portal is a centralized digital platform launched by the Department of Information Technology & Communication (DoIT&C), Rajasthan. It allows citizens to access a wide range of government applications using a single username and password.

By integrating various departments, the state has simplified the way residents interact with the government, ensuring transparency and reducing the need for physical visits to offices.

How to Register for SSO ID

If you are a new user, follow these steps to create your SSO ID:

  • Visit the official website sso.rajasthan.gov.in.
  • Click on the 'Registration' tab.
  • Select 'Citizen', 'Udhyog', or 'Govt. Employee'.
  • Use Jan Aadhaar or Google Account for authentication.
  • Complete the OTP verification sent to your mobile.
  • Choose a unique SSO ID and set your password.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Can I change my SSO ID?
No, the SSO ID once created cannot be changed. However, you can update your profile details.

2. How to recover a forgotten SSO ID?
Send an SMS "RJ SSO" to 9223166166 from your registered mobile number.

3. Is there any fee for registration?
No, creating an SSO ID is completely free for all citizens of Rajasthan.
